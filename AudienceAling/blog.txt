Tone of Voice: Informative and authoritative, yet accessible. It should be engaging enough to keep the reader interested.
Ideal Length: Typically, 500-2000 words. Longer posts (1000+ words) are good for detailed explorations of topics and SEO purposes.
Key Elements: Headings and subheadings for easy navigation, keywords for SEO, visuals, and a clear introduction and conclusion. Links to other relevant content and a CTA are also important.
